# 🚗 OmniDrive.co.ke Vision

## Our Vision

**To become Kenya's most trusted and comprehensive online vehicle marketplace, connecting buyers to their dream vehicles seamlessly—any make, any origin, delivered to their doorstep.**

---

## The Problem We Solve

1. **Fragmented Market**: Buyers must visit multiple dealerships, websites, and physical lots to find options
2. **Hidden Costs**: Import duties, shipping, and taxes are often unclear until purchase
3. **Trust Gap**: Uncertainty about vehicle history, condition, and dealer reliability
4. **Payment Barriers**: Limited local payment options for international vehicle purchases

---

## Our Solution

OmniDrive is a **one-stop digital dealership** that brings the entire vehicle buying experience online:

### 🌍 Global Inventory
- Access to vehicles from Japan, Germany, USA, UK, South Korea, and more
- Cars, bikes, buses, trucks, vans, scooters—all categories
- Real-time pricing in multiple currencies including KES

### 💳 Local Payments
- MPesa integration for instant mobile money payments
- Credit/Debit card support (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX)
- Bank transfer options
- Installment/financing calculators

### 📦 Full Service
- Import duty calculator
- Shipping logistics
- VIN history checks
- Order tracking
- Dealer locator

### 🔐 Trust Features
- User authentication
- Verified vehicle inspections
- Price alerts
- Wishlist with persistence
- Referral program
